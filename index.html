MBA Alumni Weekend in Berlin

20th June 2026

We are excited to invite you to the second alumni-organized MBA Alumni Weekend in Berlin on June 20th.

Reconnect with fellow alumni, share professional experiences, exchange ideas, establish a strong and ever-growing network of alumni, develop initiatives to support active students.

Pricing and Conditions

Participation requires a contribution fee of €35 per person. The fee includes catering during lunch break and snacks during coffee breaks.
The fee must be paid during registration and is generally non-refundable. Any surplus funds will be donated to a charity selected by the organisers, preferably the BPS Alumni Association once established.
Participation is limited and allocated on a first-come, first-served basis. Registration and payment alone do not guarantee participation; if capacity is exceeded, payments will be fully refunded.
Participation is restricted to BPS alumni, current BPS students, members of BPS, and guests approved by the organisers.
Tickets are transferable, but each person may hold only one ticket.
Registration closed 30th May 2026.
